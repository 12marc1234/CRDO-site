About CRDO

CRDO is a social running app that turns your daily runs into achievements and connections. Build streaks, unlock achievements, and network with fellow runners to stay motivated and accountable.

Features

  • Build and maintain running streaks for motivation
  • Unlock achievements and badges for milestones
  • Connect with fellow runners and build your network
  • Share your progress and celebrate with friends

How It Works

  • Run Daily — Each run contributes to your streak and unlocks achievements.
  • Build Streaks — Maintain daily runs to build impressive streaks and earn rewards.
  • Unlock Achievements — Earn badges and milestones for your running accomplishments.
  • Connect & Network — Find running buddies, share progress, and stay motivated together.
