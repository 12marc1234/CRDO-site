About CRDO

CRDO is a gamified running experience where your daily runs build a personalized digital city. Stay motivated through streaks, city upgrades, and social exploration.

Features

  • Daily running fuels your virtual city growth
  • Motivation through streaks & milestones
  • Explore and admire friends’ cities

How It Works

  • Run Daily — Each run adds resources to your city.
  • Earn Streaks — Keep your streak alive to unlock rare upgrades.
  • Build Your City — Spend your energy on buildings, landmarks, or cosmetics.
  • Explore Others — Visit friends’ cities and challenge them in run goals.

Frequently Asked Questions

When will CRDO be launched?

The app is expected to be launched on July 31st, 2025.

Is CRDO free?

The app will require a subscription. When released, it will cost $5.99/month or $49.99/year. However, right now, pre-orders are discounted.

What platforms will CRDO be available on?

CRDO will launch on iOS only. We may expand if there is enough interest.

Do I need a smartwatch or fitness tracker?

No. Your phone’s GPS is enough to track your runs and grow your city.

Do I need to run outside?

Yes — outdoor runs are required because CRDO uses GPS to map your paths.

Can I use CRDO with friends?

Yes! You can view their cities, compete on streaks, and stay motivated together.
